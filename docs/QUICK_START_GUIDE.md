# Quick Start Guide - Getting Started with OmniFlow

Welcome to OmniFlow! This guide will help you get up and running in just a few minutes.

## Your First Steps

### 1. Complete the Welcome Wizard

When you first log in, you'll see a friendly setup wizard that walks you through:
- Setting up your business profile
- Connecting your marketing tools (email & SMS)
- Adding your first contact
- Learning about AI features

**Tip:** The wizard only takes 2-3 minutes and helps you get everything configured correctly.

### 2. Connect Your Marketing Tools

To send emails and text messages, you'll need to connect your accounts:

**For Email (Brevo):**
- Go to Settings â†’ API Keys
- Click "Add Brevo API Key"
- Paste your Brevo API key
- That's it! You can now send email campaigns

**For SMS (Twilio):**
- Go to Settings â†’ API Keys
- Click "Add Twilio Credentials"
- Enter your Account SID and Auth Token
- Done! You can now send text messages

**Don't have these accounts yet?** No problem! The platform guides you through getting free accounts.

### 3. Add Your First Contacts

There are two ways to add contacts:

**Quick Add (one at a time):**
1. Go to "Leads (CRM)"
2. Click "Add Lead"
3. Fill in name, email, and phone
4. Save

**Bulk Import (many at once):**
1. Go to "Leads (CRM)"
2. Click "Import Leads"
3. Upload your CSV file or connect your CRM
4. Review and import

### 4. Send Your First Message

**Email Campaign:**
1. Go to "Email Marketing" â†’ "Create Campaign"
2. Choose a template or write your own
3. Select your contacts
4. Preview and send

**Text Message:**
1. Go to "SMS Marketing" â†’ "Send New SMS"
2. Write your message (or use AI to help)
3. Pick who to send to
4. Send

### 5. Try the AI Tools

OmniFlow has powerful AI tools to save you time:

**AI Content Generator:**
- Go to "Content Factory"
- Describe what you need (social post, email, blog)
- Let AI write it for you
- Edit and use

**AI Business Strategy:**
- Go to "AI Assistant"
- Answer a few questions about your business
- Get personalized growth recommendations

**AI Chat (Beta):**
- Go to "AI Chat"
- Ask questions about marketing, content, or business strategy
- Get instant helpful answers

## Dashboard Quick Actions
